Community Access Policy — HIGH PRIORITY

Read carefully before your shift begins

🚫 Community members are ONLY allowed Mon – Fri, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
On weekends, politely but firmly deny entry.
What to say:

"Hi there! Thank you for visiting the library today. Unfortunately, our Community Access Program is only available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. We aren't able to grant community member access on weekends.

If you're a currently enrolled student or faculty, I'd be happy to assist you. Otherwise, please feel free to come back during our community access hours — we'd love to see you then!"

If they push back:
"I completely understand, and I'm sorry for the inconvenience. This is a university policy I'm required to enforce. If you'd like to speak with a supervisor, you're welcome to contact us Monday morning."
